Tracing Two Decades of Peril: Women and Crime in India

Crime and Population Analysis in India (2001-2021)

Overview

This webpage provides a visual representation and analysis of two critical aspects of India's socio-economic landscape from 2001 to 2021:

  1. Crime Against Women
  2. Population Growth

By exploring these datasets, the goal is to understand the patterns and correlations, if any, between these two factors.
